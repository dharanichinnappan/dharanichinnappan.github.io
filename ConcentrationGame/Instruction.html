About Game

        Concentration, also known as Match Match, Match Up, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.It requires observation, concentration and a good memory to win.

Instruction:

  1. The cards are shuffled for each game
  2. You have to find the matching cards
  3. Click on any two cards
  4. The cards will be flipped and the image under the card will be shown
  5. If two cards match,those two cards will be taken from table
  6. If two cards does not match,it will be flipped back
  7. Continue untill you find all the matching cards
  8. Once you collect all the matching cards,the total moves you took to match all the cards will be shown
  9. You can reset/start a new game by clicking on START NEW GAME button
